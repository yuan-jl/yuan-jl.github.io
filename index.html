About

设计师往往会有自己的偏好
我发现我对于红色和绿色有着不同寻常的执念
两种对比色往往给予人冲击力，不过时常也会因搭配不当而翻车
我试图用绛红和墨绿创造高级感，融入藏青色作调节
用这三种颜色创造了这片天地，同时也尝试对自己的作品做了分类

Designers often have their own preferences.
I found that I had an unusual obsession with red and green.
Two contrasting colors often impact people, however bad use will lead to disaster.
I tried to create an advanced feeling with crimson and copper green,
adding Tibetan cyan as a fusion agent.
I Created this personal website with these three colors, and classified my works at the same time.

Classification



红 Crimson

红色是蕴藏在每一个种花家心中的一抹定色，生于爱与关怀。
It is important for Chinese,which symbolizes love and care.



蓝 Tibetan cyan

蓝色是深邃海底的浪漫，也是朗日天空的澄澈，蓝是纯净，蓝是无穷。
It is the romance of the deep sea and the clarity of the sunny sky,signifying pure and infinite.



绿 Copper green

万物生于绿，归于绿，绿色带来和平与健康，绿色焕发生命与希望。
Green connects everything,which radiates peace and health as well as life and hope.



未知 Unknow
